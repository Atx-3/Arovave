About Us

Who We Are

Arovave Global is a professionally managed export company committed to delivering high quality Indian products to global markets with transparency, speed, and trust.

Our Legacy

Backed by two well-established Indian companies — Raj Prints (25+ years in advertising & branding) and Vigilant Life Sciences Pvt. Ltd. (pharmaceutical expertise) — we bring decades of operational excellence and market knowledge to the international stage.

Our Mission

To connect Indian manufacturing excellence with global demand by offering curated, compliant, and value-driven exports — supported by real-time communication, ethical practices, and long-term partnerships.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as a trusted Indian export house that redefines reliability, transparency, and quality in international trade.
